DISEC

Agenda: To Be Declared

Background Guide: To Be Released

“Days are gone forever where our enemies could blackmail us with nuclear bombs”

The Disarmament and International Security Committee is the first of the six committees of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA). It considers all matters of international peace, security and disarmament, regulating armament and strengthening stability among nations.
DISEC deals with disarmament, global challenges and threats to peace that affect the international community and seeks out solutions to the challenges in the international security regime.
Stay tuned, as this is just the beginning of the intriguing lineup of committees coming your way.
